G Foundation

Full Title: Fieldwork Observation & Unveiling Novel Data & Anomaly Tracking for Investigation Outlier Networks
Designation: Universe Ranking #4 Organization
Founded By: Geneti Phulst (The Alternate Scientist)
Origin: Prehistoric Era of the Old Universe
Headquarters: Mobile — Main Hubship known as Foundation Ark
Leader: Geneti Phulst (G)
Core Motto: “No anomaly without knowledge, no knowledge without foundation.”

Overview

The G Foundation is one of the most powerful, feared, and respected organizations in the Universe, ranked #4 in influence. Established by Geneti Phulst, it was born from his vision to catalog, protect, and weaponize the anomalous truths of reality.

Originally a small research society hidden within the Old Universe, the Foundation evolved into a multiversal powerhouse, commanding both scientific authority and military might. Its purpose is not only academic — while its researchers collect and analyze anomalies, its forces are also tasked with defending the Alternate race, eradicating hostile forces, and eliminating threats beyond mortal comprehension.

To many, the G Foundation is salvation; to others, it is a cold, terrifying machine of control.

Core Responsibilities

  • Anomaly Research & Containment — Studying unstable phenomena, rogue lifeforms, forbidden technologies, and cosmic irregularities.
  • Entity Classification — Cataloging universal beings, from soul-lifeforms to forbidden gods.
  • Data Acquisition & Analysis — Extracting knowledge from both physical and metaphysical sources.
  • Multiversal Security — Deploying task forces to prevent disasters across realms.
  • Alternate Preservation — Protecting the dwindling Alternate Race from extinction.
  • Judicial Elimination — Eradicating entities deemed “irreversibly hostile.”
  • Information Control — Controlling the flow of cosmic truths to avoid panic or misuse.
  • Experimental Projects — Creating weapons, technologies, and alternate realities.

Subforces & Divisions

  1. The Researchers — “The eye of G.” Specialists deployed across the Universe, disguised as civilians, scholars, or wanderers; they catalog anomalies and observe first, interfere later.
  2. Allocation Task Force (ATF) — “The Sword of G.” Primary military arm; handles large-scale containment breaches and eradication campaigns.
  3. H.O.R.R.O.R. — “The Shield of G.” Elite guards and enforcers stationed at the Foundation Ark and dimensional bases.
  4. E.Y.E.S. — “The Ears of G.” Intelligence-gathering force intercepting transmissions and monitoring anomalies.
  5. The Creed — “The Shadow of G.” Covert assassination division executing silent eliminations to protect secrecy.

Hierarchy & Command

Geneti Phulst (G) — Supreme Leader, “The Alternate Scientist.” Senior Level Council (The Broskis) — veteran leaders chosen by G. Division Heads — commanders with absolute control. Operatives — researchers, soldiers, agents trained under Foundation doctrine.

Legacy & Infamy

The G Foundation is both a sanctuary and a nightmare. To the Alternate Race, it is the only beacon of survival; to the Cultivators, it is a bloody warning — the Holocaust of Cultivation incident remains burned into memory. Despite controversy, its influence is undeniable — no empire, sect, or civilization dares underestimate it.

G基金会

全称： Fieldwork Observation & Unveiling Novel Data & Anomaly Tracking for Investigation Outlier Networks
宇宙排名： #4 组织
创立者： 杰尼提 · 法斯特（G）
起源： 旧宇宙时期
总部： 机动型 —— 主舰「基金会方舟」
领袖： 杰尼提 · 法斯特（G）
核心信条： “没有异常就没有知识，没有知识就没有根基。”

概览

G基金会 是宇宙中最强大、最令人畏惧与尊敬的组织之一，位列第四。由 杰尼提 · 法斯特 创立，基金会的诞生源于他对 宇宙异常现象的研究、保护与武装化 的宏愿。

最初，它只是旧宇宙中的一个隐秘学术团体，但随着时间推移，基金会逐渐发展为横跨多元宇宙的庞然大物，既掌握 科学权威，也拥有 军事霸权。基金会的使命不仅仅是学术性的。除了研究和记录异常，它的武装力量也负责 守护伪人族、清剿敌对势力、消灭超越理解的威胁。

对一些人而言，基金会是救赎；对另一些人而言，它是冰冷恐怖的掌控机器。

核心职责

  • 异常研究与收容 —— 调查不稳定现象、异种生命、禁忌科技与宇宙异象。
  • 实体分类 —— 为宇宙级生命、灵魂体、禁忌神明建立档案。
  • 数据采集与解析 —— 从物质与形而上来源中提取信息。
  • 多元宇宙安保 —— 派遣特遣部队防止灾难蔓延。
  • 伪人保全 —— 保护濒临灭绝的伪人族群。
  • 裁决消灭 —— 根除被判定为“无法挽回敌对”的存在。
  • 信息管控 —— 控制宇宙真相的流动，防止恐慌与滥用。
  • 实验项目 —— 开发武器、科技，以及平行现实。

主要分部与小队

  1. The Researchers（搜探者） — “G之眼。” 遍布宇宙的观察者，常伪装成平民或学者，负责记录异常。
  2. Allocation Task Force (ATF)（分配特遣队） — “G之剑.” 基金会主力军队，执行大规模收容与歼灭战。
  3. H.O.R.R.O.R. — “G之盾.” 基金会精英守卫，常驻基金会方舟与异维基地。
  4. E.Y.E.S. — “G之耳.” 情报收集部门，负责截获通讯并监控异常。
  5. The Creed（信条） — “G之影.” 暗杀分部，秘密清除威胁基金会的人。

架构與指揮體系

杰尼提 · 法斯特（G） —— 至高领袖。高级理事会 —— 由G亲自挑选的元老。分部首领与行动员 —— 受基金会严格训练并执行命令。

遗产与恶名

G基金会 同时是 庇护所与梦魇。对于伪人族群它是希望；对于修士它是血的警示 —— 「修真大屠杀事件」永刻史册。无论赞颂或诅咒，其影响力皆不可忽视。
