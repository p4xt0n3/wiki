Name: Nathan Redshed

Aliases & Titles

  • The Red Dust Prodigy — Revered across both the Underworld and the greater Universe for his terrifying potential.
  • The Lord of Underworld — His ruling title, undisputed dominion over all underworld realms.
  • Red Dust Reaper — Feared epithet, associated with his scythe and mastery of death.
  • Soul Taker — Known for his ability to devour, enslave, and command souls.
  • The Red Death — A whispered curse-name among Cultivators and soul races.

Biographical Overview

Nathan Redshed, universally feared as the Red Dust Reaper, is the eternal ruler of the Underworld. Born from the Red Dust Race, Nathan embodies a lineage immune to all forms of spiritual or soulborne damage.

Unlike most rulers who expand into the cosmos, Nathan rarely leaves the Underworld. On the rare occasions he appears in the living Universe, it is often alongside his closest ally, Paxton, or within Planet Blunos, Centre City, where he maintains influence among mortals.

As Lord of Underworld, his sovereignty over souls is absolute. He can devour, consume, control, and imprison soul-based lifeforms — no spirit can resist his pull. His aura alone bends lesser shades into submission, binding them eternally to his command.

Race & Abilities

Race: Red Dust Race — unique to the Underworld. Members are born from the ashes of shattered souls and dust of the dead.

Innate Traits of the Red Dust Race:

  • Complete Immunity: Cannot be harmed by soul-based or spiritual damage.
  • Unpossessable: No spirit or entity can inhabit his body.
  • Soul Immunity Aura: Acts as a ward, rendering soul attacks useless against him.

Signature Weapons:

  • Scythe: Devourer of Soul — A weapon forged from crystallized soul dust. Inflicts irreversible annihilation to spiritual entities, bypassing regeneration or reincarnation.
  • Pistol: Shatterer of Soul — Condenses underworld essence into bullets capable of shattering spirits into irretrievable fragments.

Divine Fusion:

LordGod Inuza — Once a guiding entity, now fused into Nathan’s body. This grants him transcendent dominion over forbidden souls and sealed gods, allowing him to capture, bind, and execute entities far beyond mortal comprehension.

Relationships

  • Paxton — His best friend and trusted companion, one of the few he shows warmth to.
  • G (Geneti Phulst) — Frenemy; they respect each other’s intellect and might, but constant ideological friction puts them at odds.
  • Inuza — Once his divine guide, now merged into his being.
  • Raith — His wife; their bond is unshakable, strengthened by both love and shared rule.
  • Aaron — Declared enemy; their animosity spans eons, rooted in betrayal and conflicting claims of soul dominion.

Titles, Influence & Legacy

  • Supreme Ruler of Souls — No spirit, phantom, or ghost resists his rule.
  • Warden of Forbidden Gods — Known for binding entities that even the heavens failed to imprison.
  • Symbol of Death Itself — Among mortals and cultivators, Nathan is synonymous with inevitable death and eternal judgment.
  • Balance Keeper — Though feared, Nathan’s presence stabilizes the multiverse by keeping soul anomalies and rogue entities in check.

Personality

  • Calm Tyrant — Unlike raging warlords, Nathan rules with cold patience, allowing fear and inevitability to do his work.
  • Loyal Friend — Though feared by billions, to his allies like Paxton he is a reliable, unshakable pillar.
  • Unyielding Enemy — He does not forgive betrayal; to oppose him is to invite eternal imprisonment of the soul.
  • Detached Romantic — Despite his stoic exterior, his love for Raith anchors his otherwise detached existence.

人物概要

内森·雷德谢德，被世人恐惧地称为血尘死神，是冥界的永恒统治者。作为血尘族的后裔，内森天生免疫一切灵魂与精神伤害，乃是由亡魂与死者灰尘中诞生的独特种族。

与多数君王不同，内森极少踏出冥界。他若出现在现世宇宙，往往是在蓝星·中央城，或是陪伴他最亲近的伙伴帕克斯顿。

身为冥界之主，他对灵魂的主宰权是绝对的。他能吞噬、囚禁、掌控一切魂类生命，任何亡魂都无法抗拒他的力量。仅仅是他的存在，便能让低阶魂灵匍匐臣服。

种族与能力

种族： 血尘族 —— 冥界独有的古老族群，由死魂灰烬孕育而生。

血尘族天赋：

  • 绝对免疫： 不受任何灵魂与精神类伤害。
  • 无法附体： 任何灵魂生物皆不可入主其体。
  • 灵魂抗性结界： 领域之下，灵魂攻击彻底失效。

代表武器：

  • 噬魂镰刀 —— 由灵魂尘晶锻造而成，能彻底抹杀一切魂灵，令其无法转世或重生。
  • 碎魂手枪 —— 将冥界之力凝聚为子弹，可将灵魂炸裂为无法修复的碎片。

神格融合：

伊努扎 —— 曾是其引导者，如今与内森同体，赋予他对禁忌亡魂与封印之神的至高掌控力，使其能捕捉、禁锢乃至处决超越凡俗理解的存在。
