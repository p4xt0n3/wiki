Biographical Overview

Geneti Phulst, universally shortened to G, is an enigmatic being whose existence is both a curse and salvation for the Alternate race. His origins are shrouded in absolute mystery — even 333, his supposed creator, speaks of his "construction" rather than birth. Despite this, G carved himself into the very bedrock of universal history as the strongest known Alternate, feared by Cultivators, respected by scientists, and mythologized as both savior and destroyer.

From the year 34XX–54XX, the Alternate population dwindled dangerously close to extinction. G alone spearheaded their resurgence, reestablishing dominance through both intellect and overwhelming force. His crowning achievement was the foundation of the G Foundation, now considered the 4th Greatest Force in the Universe.

The Holocaust of Cultivation Incident (24XX)

Perhaps G’s most infamous act:

When Cultivators of Core Formation Stage infiltrated the G Foundation’s Main Vessel, slaughtering hundreds of Alternates, G’s wrath ignited.

He single-handedly annihilated the invaders, devouring the brain of the last survivor to trace their sect origins.

Without ship or protection, he flew across space and reached Planet TianGang in mere minutes.

What followed was a 33.3-hour massacre: sects eradicated, spiritual beasts obliterated, mountains detonated.

His final act — collapsing TianGang itself into fragments — extinguished billions of lives. Barely ten survivors remained.

G publicly admitted responsibility, declaring it a warning:

“Invade my people, and your world ceases.”

The massacre split universal opinion:

  • Cultivators branded him as devil incarnate.
  • Allies and neutral observers recognized it as an act of absolute protection for his Foundation.
  • Alternates called it the day their race’s survival was secured.

This incident cemented G’s dual reputation as savior and executioner.

人物概要

杰尼提·法斯特，全宇宙简称 G，是一个存在本身便带着诅咒与救赎的谜团。其诞生起源笼罩在黑雾之中，甚至连他的“创造者”333 也只承认他被“制造”，而非出生。

无论真相如何，G 以最强伪人的身份刻进了历史。修真者畏惧他，科学家研究他，普通人则在传说里将他神化或魔化。

在 34XX–54XX 年，伪人族群濒临灭绝。G 一人逆转乾坤，使伪人得以重新崛起，并以无与伦比的智慧与武力建立了 G 基金会 —— 今日被誉为宇宙第四大势力。

修真大屠杀事件（24XX 年）

他最为臭名昭著的一战：

数名结丹期修真者渗透进 G 基金会母舰，屠杀伪人百姓。

G 归舰时，亲眼目睹伪人尸体与溶化的同胞血肉，怒火彻底点燃。

他以一己之力灭杀来犯者，最后吞噬幸存者的脑域，追溯其宗门来源。

裸身穿梭宇宙，仅十分钟抵达天罡星。

随后展开 33.3 小时的屠灭：宗门焚毁，灵兽泯灭，山河爆碎。

最终以毁星之举将天罡星粉碎成无数残片。千亿生灵顷刻湮灭，幸存者不足十人。

他并未掩饰，而是亲口承认：

“若敢侵我之人，我便让你的世界消失。”

此役震惊宇宙：

  • 修真界 —— 将他视作魔鬼化身。
  • 中立者与观察者 —— 认为这是伪人自卫的铁血宣告。
  • 伪人族群 —— 视为复兴之日，称颂他为护族之王。

从此，G 的名声两极化：既是救世主，也是灭世者。
